THE SENA INSURANCE PLC

Sena Insurance PLC (SIPLC) is a leading general insurer in Bangladesh, offering a wide range of insurance products including fire, motor, marine, travel, engineering, aviation, and miscellaneous insurance. They are committed to innovation and customer satisfaction, ensuring accessibility both physically and virtually. SIPLC continuously strives to improve its services, maintaining high standards of corporate practices and transparency. Their mission is to become a leading insurance company through innovative products and customer-friendly services, ensuring profit for sponsors and shareholders. Their vision is to be a viably profitable insurance company, providing exceptional value to their customers and stakeholders.
